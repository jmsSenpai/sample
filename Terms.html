Terms of Service

Welcome to the Datamex College of Saint Adeline Online Lost & Found System. By using this system, you agree to comply with the following terms of service, which ensure a secure and efficient experience for all users.

1. General Use

The Online Lost & Found System is designed exclusively for students, faculty, and staff of Datamex College of Saint Adeline, Caloocan, to report and retrieve lost or found items within the campus.

  • Only registered users with valid school credentials may access the system.
  • Users must provide accurate information when reporting lost or found items, including item descriptions and images where applicable.
  • Unauthorized access or misuse of the system is strictly prohibited.

2. Reporting and Claiming Items

Users may report lost or found items through the system or by submitting items to a designated school administrator for entry into the system.

  • All reports are subject to verification by system administrators.
  • Claims for found items must be verified by providing sufficient proof of ownership, as determined by the administrator.
  • The system does not guarantee the recovery of lost items but aims to facilitate the process.

3. System Limitations

The system is designed for internal use within Datamex College of Saint Adeline and has specific limitations as outlined in the project scope.

  • The system does not support mobile apps or external integrations.
  • Local storage may limit scalability; users are advised to report issues promptly.
  • The system does not address theft prevention or dishonest reporting.

4. User Responsibilities

Users are expected to use the system responsibly and ethically.

  • Do not submit false or misleading reports.
  • Respect the property of others when using the system.
  • Report any technical issues or suspicious activity to the system administrator immediately.

5. Changes to Terms

Datamex College of Saint Adeline reserves the right to update these terms of service at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or system announcements.

6. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding these terms, please contact the system administrator at support@datamex.edu.ph.
